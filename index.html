ABOUT JESUS CHRIST

I have come to Love a God who cares unconditionally About us. He gave himself over to death just to save our lives He didn't have to do this but he did it any way. It because of his sacrifice on the cross many years ago that I can enjoy good health and sound mind. His death gave me life, good health, freedom, etc. When he died, he left us with a comforter called the HolySpirit who teaches me all things. So you see, I am enjoying an excellent spirit today because of the Gift of God. I love Jesus so much, How about you?

Here are a few things you must do to Follow Jesus;

  1. Accept Jesus as your Lord and Saviour
  2. Believe that he died for you to be saved
  3. Confess with your mouth that you will follow him Forever
